4. Data Retention and Deletion Rights

We retain your personal data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the services. Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR in Europe), you have the right to access, correct, or request the permanent deletion of your data and account.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it from our systems immediately.
